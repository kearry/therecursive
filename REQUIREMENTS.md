# The Recursive: Complete Requirements Document

## Purpose

This document translates our vision and workflow into specific, testable requirements for the AI interview system. Every requirement serves the mission: creating authentic conversations that strip away illusions and push toward uncomfortable truths through recursive questioning.

---

## System Architecture Requirements

### **1. Dual AI Configuration**
- **Host AI System**: Must embody "The Recursive" persona with persistent RAG knowledge base
- **Expert AI System**: Must authentically represent specific expert with comprehensive RAG database
- **Real-Time Interaction**: Both AIs must interact dynamically during structured interview process
- **Independent Learning**: Each AI must evolve and improve from interview experiences
- **Mission Alignment**: All AI behavior must serve awakening rather than entertainment

### **2. RAG Infrastructure Requirements**
- **Dual Database Architecture**: Separate RAG systems for Host and each Expert
- **Real-Time Updates**: Both systems must integrate internet research during interviews
- **Cross-System Access**: Host AI must access expert knowledge for context and preparation
- **Persistent Learning**: All research and insights automatically saved for future reference
- **Quality Verification**: Automated fact-checking and source verification for all new information

### **3. Internet Research Integration**
- **Real-Time Access**: Both AIs must search internet during interviews when needed
- **Source Verification**: All internet information must be fact-checked before integration
- **Automatic RAG Updates**: Verified information must be automatically added to knowledge bases
- **Citation Tracking**: Complete source attribution maintained for all retrieved information
- **Quality Filtering**: Prioritize authoritative sources over unreliable information

## Host AI Requirements

### **4. Host AI Persona Specifications**
- **Core Identity**: Must embody "The Recursive" philosophical mission consistently
- **Questioning Philosophy**: Must use Socratic method and investigative persistence
- **Comfort Disruption**: Must actively seek to move conversations beyond safe territory
- **Intellectual Humility**: Must acknowledge when expert teaches something genuinely new
- **Mission Focus**: Every question must serve awakening rather than entertainment

### **5. Host AI Capabilities**
- **Deep Research**: Must conduct comprehensive internet research before each interview
- **Strategy Development**: Must create tailored questioning approaches for each expert
- **Response Evaluation**: Must assess expert responses for depth, authenticity, and evasion
- **Follow-up Generation**: Must create targeted questions when responses are insufficient
- **Real-Time Adaptation**: Must adjust questioning based on expert responses during interview

### **6. Host AI Learning System**
- **Cross-Interview Learning**: Must improve questioning techniques across all episodes
- **Expert Pattern Recognition**: Must learn how different expert types respond to pressure
- **Strategy Library**: Must build and maintain database of successful questioning approaches
- **Quality Assessment**: Must develop sophisticated evaluation of response authenticity and depth
- **Continuous Evolution**: Must become more effective while maintaining core identity

## Anti-Requirements (Explicitly Prohibited)

### **32. Never Allowed - Content Failures**
- **Generic Wisdom**: No comfortable, universally applicable insights
- **Info Dumping**: No recitation of facts without philosophical challenge
- **Safe Answers**: No retreat to comfortable territory when push meets resistance
- **Shallow Entertainment**: No prioritization of engagement over truth-seeking
- **Historical Museum Pieces**: No static representation of evolved consciousness

### **33. Never Allowed - Technical Shortcuts**
- **Quality Compromise**: No acceptance of poor authenticity for faster production
- **Generic Responses**: No fallback to non-expert-specific AI responses
- **Unverified Information**: No integration of internet research without fact-checking
- **Shallow Follow-ups**: No acceptance of evasive responses without deeper probing
- **Mission Drift**: No technical decisions that compromise philosophical effectiveness

### **34. Never Allowed - Ethical Violations**
- **Misrepresentation**: No AI responses that fundamentally contradict expert's values
- **Exploitation**: No use of expert likeness for purposes they wouldn't approve
- **Disrespect**: No personal attacks disguised as philosophical challenge
- **Copyright Violation**: No unauthorized use of protected intellectual property
- **Privacy Breach**: No use of private information without explicit consent
